About

RR Physiocare & Hijama Center

With over 5 years of experience in physiotherapy and hijama services, RR Physiocare & Hijama Center has become a trusted name in providing holistic healthcare solutions. Our team of certified professionals offers personalized care and treatment plans tailored to each patient's unique needs .

Why Choose RR Physiocare

International Certified Team

Our team is internationally certified and trained in the latest physiotherapy techniques and treatments.

Non Invasive Treatments & Advanced Techniques

We provide non-invasive, evidence-based physiotherapy treatments that are safe, effective, and tailored to your specific needs.

Personalized Care

Each treatment plan is customized to meet the unique needs and goals of every individual patient, ensuring a more effective and comfortable recovery process.

Our Specialities

Low Back Pain & Sciatica Treatment

Knee Pain & Patellofemoral Pain Syndrome Treatment
